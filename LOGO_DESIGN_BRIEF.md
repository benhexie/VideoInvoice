# SnapQuote — Logo Design Brief

## App Overview

**SnapQuote** is an AI-powered quoting and invoicing app for iOS and Android. The core experience is dead simple: a contractor, tradesperson, or freelancer records a short video (or types a description) of a job, and the AI instantly generates a professional, itemized quote. The app also supports uploading price lists and exporting polished PDF invoices.

**Target users:** Contractors, builders, plumbers, electricians, landscapers, freelancers — anyone who needs to turn a site walkthrough or a quick description into a professional quote in under a minute.

**Brand positioning:** Fast, intelligent, professional. Not a generic invoicing tool — an AI assistant that does the heavy lifting so tradespeople can focus on the work.

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

These are the exact brand colors from the app — the logo must align with them.

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary accent** | `#4F46E5` | Main CTA buttons, active states — the brand color |
| **Accent light** | `#818CF8` | Highlights, glow effects, AI processing indicator |
| **Dark background** | `#09090B` | Near-black — primary dark mode bg |
| **Light background** | `#FAFAFA` | Off-white — primary light mode bg |
| **Success green** | `#10B981` | Confirmed states |
| **Surface dark** | `#18181B` | Cards on dark bg |

The logo must look great on **both** `#09090B` (dark) and `#FAFAFA` (light) backgrounds. Provide separate lockups for each.

### Typography Direction

- The wordmark **"SnapQuote"** should feel **modern and geometric** — think rounded sans-serif (similar to Inter, Satoshi, or Geist).
- Weight: **Bold** for "Snap", slightly lighter or same for "Quote" — or treat it as a single bold wordmark with a color split between the two words.
- No scripts, no serifs, no vintage styles.
- Optional: "AI" or a small "ai" badge can be incorporated subtly if it doesn't clutter the mark.

### Icon / Symbol Concept

The in-app identity already uses a **lightning bolt (Zap)** icon in `#4F46E5` as the "SnapQuote AI" badge. The logo mark should build on or complement this direction.

**Core ideas to explore:**

1. **Lightning bolt + document** — A zap bolt overlapping or cutting through a page/invoice corner. Communicates instant + professional.
2. **Camera shutter + quote block** — A stylized shutter aperture framing a price tag or document. Captures the "snap" (photo/video) + "quote" duality.
3. **Spark / node mark** — An abstract node with radiating lines suggesting AI intelligence and speed. Clean, scalable, tech-forward.
4. **Bold "S" lettermark** — A custom "S" incorporating a bolt or upward-moving form. Works well as an app icon at small sizes.

The icon should be **bold enough to read at 20px** (tab bar) and **striking at 1024px** (app store). Avoid thin lines that disappear at small sizes.

## Tone & Style

**Do:** Clean, modern, confident, minimal, tech-forward, slightly bold.

**Don't:** Cartoonish, overly corporate/enterprise, handcrafted/rustic, gradients that look like 2015 SaaS, clipart document icons.

**References / mood:**
- Linear, Vercel, Raycast (clean dark-mode-first product design)
- Stripe (professional, trustworthy, modern financial tool)
- Notion's simplicity, but with more energy/urgency (it's a *snap*)

---

## App Icon Specs (iOS / Android)

The app icon is the most important deliverable. It will be cropped to a **rounded rectangle (iOS)** or **circle (Android)**.

- **Background:** Either solid `#4F46E5` (indigo) or `#09090B` (near black)
- **Icon mark:** White or `#818CF8` symbol, centered, occupying ~60% of the canvas
- The icon should feel at home next to apps like Stripe, Linear, and Notion on a phone home screen
- No text in the app icon — symbol only

---

## What to Avoid

- Generic invoice/receipt clip art
- Overly literal camera + document combos that look stock
- Rainbow or multi-gradient color schemes — the palette is intentionally restrained
- Drop shadows or heavy bevels
- Thin strokes below 2px at any intended display size

## Questions for the Designer

1. Do you want to incorporate "AI" into the wordmark (e.g. "SnapQuote **AI**") or keep it clean as "SnapQuote"?
2. Should the icon lean into the **video/camera** metaphor (the primary input method) or the **invoice/document** metaphor (the output)?
3. Preference on icon background: solid indigo `#4F46E5` or near-black `#09090B`?
